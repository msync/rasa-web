Milestone 3 planned

Value authority and mutability safety kernel

One affine cell/handle/store mechanism carries owner, extent, generation, mutability, escape, movement, publication, cleanup, and synchronization authority. Concrete Rust cells are non-clonable; handles are aliases; transfer invalidates old handles and rehomes cleanup atomically. Ordinary vector-of construction remains the first shift-left refinement: checked i64/f64 contracts never imply packed storage. Open or unproved authority cannot admit mutation, movement, sharing, region allocation, or tasks. RESET-0007 consumes this kernel before further concurrency or capability movement.
