Milestone 11 planned

WMP-2 image data-plane proof

The packed-loop contract slice now adds examples/proofs/packed-loop/: packed-copy.ras admits the first one-dimensional u8 WMP-2 packed-linear candidate through compiler-recognized rasa.array intrinsics, ^:rasa/packed, induction-indexed read/write, and array/persistent!; buffer-escape-refused.ras, index-shape-refused.ras, counted-refused.ras, and refused.ras keep the strict refusal path; inferred.ras preserves unfenced analyzer-driven optimization. Remaining pressure: specific escape/sealing diagnostics, broader dtypes and shapes, K-mer bitmask range facts, Sobel affine indexed-buffer facts, browser execution track, guard placement, boundary movement reporting from compiler facts, and measured fallback comparison.
